Sauce Pancake

Ingredients

  • Oil

  • Onion

  • Garlic

  • Bean paste

  • Oyster sauce

  • Chinese Five Spices

  • Cumin Powder

  • White seasame

  • Starch

  • Chinese scallion pancake

  • Water

Procedures

  1. Put oil in a pan, when it is slightly hot, add chopped green onion and saute until fragrant

  2. Add one teaspon of each bean paste, oyster sauce, Chinese five spices, cumin powder, white seasame, starch , half of teaspon of sugar, and water to boil until sticky for making the sauce

  3. Pan fry the Chinese scallion pancake, turn over, until golden color on both sides

  4. Brush the pan with the sauce, sprikle with sesame seeds and chopped green onion

  5. Cut sauce pancake into small pieces as you like for convenience
