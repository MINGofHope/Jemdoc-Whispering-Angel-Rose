About me

As a budding data scientist, I place great importance on fostering a unique research perspective, sharpening analytical skills, promoting knowledge sharing, and maintaining a lifelong learning attitude. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Engineering from Qilu University of Technology followed by a Master's degree from Shandong University, Jinan, China, in 2020. In pursuit of my passion for data analysis and machine learning, I enrolled in a second Master's program at Northeastern University in September 2021. Throughout my academic career, I have gained valuable experience in diverse areas of study, including engineering, statistics, and machine learning. My diverse background has enabled me to approach research problems from various angles, providing a unique perspective on data analysis. I am driven to pursue my passion for solving real-world problems through data-driven solutions, and I am dedicated to expanding my knowledge and skills in the field of data science. I am committed to being an active member of the data science community, where I can share my knowledge and collaborate with other professionals. I believe that a strong community promotes learning and growth, which is why I am committed to giving back and supporting the growth of the next generation of data scientists.
